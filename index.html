Short Bio

  • Kai-Xuan Chen is a Ph.D candidate in the College Of Computer Science and Technology at the Zhejiang University and a member of VIPA Group, led by Prof. Mingli Song. His major research focuses on machine learning and its applications to data mining and computer vision.

  • Kai-Xuan Chen received his B.E. degree in software engineering from the Jiangsu University of Science and Technology, Zhenjiang, China, in 2016. From Sept. 2016, He started his M.S. degree in computer science under the supervision of Prof. Xiao-Jun Wu, and received the M.S. degree in computer science from Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China, in 2019.
